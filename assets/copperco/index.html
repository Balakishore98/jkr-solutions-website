Our Process

From bean to cup

01 · SOURCE

Single-origin beans from Coorg & Chikmagalur estates, picked at peak ripeness.

02 · ROAST

Small-batch roasted in-house, twice weekly, to a light-medium profile.

03 · GRIND

Ground to order per brew method — filter, pour-over, or espresso.

04 · SERVE

Brewed fresh, cup by cup — no batch-brewed pots sitting on a warmer.
