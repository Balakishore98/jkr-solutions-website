FAQ

Common questions

Do I need technical knowledge to use what you build? ▾

No. Everything is designed for non-technical owners and managers. We provide training on handover and a user guide. If anything feels confusing, we fix it.

How long does a dashboard or automation take? ▾

Starter dashboard: 5–10 working days. Full platform: 3–6 weeks. Automations: 1–3 weeks per workflow. Depends on data complexity and how quickly we get sample data from you.

My data is in old Excel files or Tally. Can you work with that? ▾

That's our speciality. We've worked with Tally, Busy, custom ERPs, and dozens of Excel formats. We clean and connect your data into a single live view.

For e-commerce, do I need AWS or a server? ▾

No. We use WooCommerce or Shopify on shared hosting (₹2,000–₹5,000/year). AWS is only needed at high traffic — we'll recommend it only if you reach that stage. Razorpay handles payments at 2% per transaction.

Can I cancel the monthly retainer? ▾

Yes — monthly, no lock-in. 15 days' notice. The one-time build fee is separate, so you own what was built even if you stop the monthly plan.
