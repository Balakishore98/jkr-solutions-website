FAQ

Common questions

Do I need technical knowledge to use what you build? ▾

No. Everything we build is designed for non-technical owners and managers. We provide training when we hand over, and a user guide. If anything feels confusing, we fix it.

How long does a dashboard or automation take to build? ▾

Starter dashboard: 5–10 working days. Full platform: 3–6 weeks. Automations: 1–3 weeks per workflow. Timeline depends on data complexity and how quickly we can get sample data from you.

What if my data is in an old accounting system or multiple Excel files? ▾

That's our speciality. We've worked with Tally, Busy, custom ERPs, and dozens of Excel formats. We clean and connect your data so it flows into a single live view.

For e-commerce, do I need AWS or a server? ▾

No. We use WooCommerce or Shopify on shared hosting (₹2,000–₹5,000/year). AWS is only needed at high traffic/volume — we'll recommend it if and when you reach that stage. Razorpay handles payments at 2% per transaction.

Can I cancel the monthly retainer? ▾

Yes — monthly, no lock-in. We just ask for 15 days' notice. The one-time build fee is separate from the retainer, so you own what was built even if you stop the monthly plan.
